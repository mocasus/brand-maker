# Kawaii Ghost Icon Prompt

**Style:** Wavy-bottom ghost silhouette, playful, minimal.
**Best for:** Playful apps, indie brands, gaming, chat apps.

## The Prompt

```
A cute and minimalist flat app icon featuring ONLY a ghost silhouette,
viewed from the front. The ghost is a soft rounded top (semi-circle
dome) transitioning into a wavy scalloped bottom edge with 3-4 gentle
undulations.

Ghost features:
- Two oversized vertical pill-shaped eyes, cut out as transparent
  negative space (background color shows through)
- Eyes occupy 25% of body height, symmetric, positioned in upper-third
- No mouth, no arms, no facial details beyond eyes

Color palette (strictly two-tone):
- Ghost body: [ICON_COLOR] — solid flat fill
- Background: [BACKGROUND_COLOR] squircle with 25-30% corner radius

Style: kawaii chibi ghost, friendly not scary, modern flat vector,
Japanese-cute meets iOS app icon aesthetic. Perfectly centered.
Symmetric vertical axis. Sharp clean vector edges. High contrast for
readability at 16px through 1024px.

--no scary details, teeth, tongue, arms, hands, chains, spooky effects,
3D shading, gradients, shadows, texture, outlines, text, watermarks,
multiple ghosts, side view
```

## Variables

| Placeholder | Description | Example |
|-------------|-------------|---------|
| `[ICON_COLOR]` | Ghost fill | `#FFFFFF`, `#E0E7FF` |
| `[BACKGROUND_COLOR]` | Squircle bg | `#6366F1`, `#0F172A` |

## Variants

- `--blush` → two small blush ovals on cheeks (lighter shade of bg)
- `--sleepy` → horizontal short eyes, half-closed
- `--waving` → tiny bump on side (implied arm wave)
