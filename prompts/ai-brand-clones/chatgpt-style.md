# ChatGPT Style Logo Prompt

**Visual DNA:** Green/teal knot flower · Geometric symmetric · 6-fold rotational symmetry · Modern flat.

**Tested on:** ChatGPT (DALL-E 3 / GPT Image)

## The Prompt

```
A flat vector app icon in the visual style of ChatGPT's brand logo.

Composition:
- Square 1:1 aspect ratio, iOS/macOS app icon proportions
- Solid [BACKGROUND_COLOR] squircle canvas with 25% corner radius
- Centered geometric knot/flower shape in [ICON_COLOR], occupying
  65-75% of the icon area

Shape design:
- 6-fold rotational symmetry (like ChatGPT's signature knot)
- Six overlapping rounded petals forming an interlocking flower/knot
- Central hexagonal negative space (empty center)
- Each petal has smooth curved edges, no sharp corners
- Petals appear to weave over/under each other (Celtic knot feel)

Color palette (strictly two-tone):
- Foreground knot: [ICON_COLOR] — solid flat fill
- Background: [BACKGROUND_COLOR] — solid, no gradient

Alternative simpler variant:
- Six-petal flower without overlapping (simpler, more readable at small sizes)
- Petals meet at central point
- Same 6-fold symmetry

Style: modern minimal tech aesthetic, geometric precision, flat vector,
mathematically symmetric, feels like AI/algorithm made visible.

--no gradients, 3D effects, shadows, textures, outlines, text,
watermarks, realistic details, mechanical parts, asymmetric elements
```

## Variables

| Placeholder | ChatGPT Default | Your Brand |
|-------------|-----------------|------------|
| `[ICON_COLOR]` | `#FFFFFF` (white) | your color |
| `[BACKGROUND_COLOR]` | `#10A37F` (ChatGPT green) | your bg |

## Signature ChatGPT Colors

```
Green:       #10A37F  (primary)
Dark green:  #0D8B6C  (variant)
Cream bg:    #F7F7F8  (light UI)
Dark:        #202123  (dark mode)
```

## Variants

**Variant A — Simple flower (recommended for readability):**
```
- Six-petal flower, petals meet at center
- No overlapping/weaving
- Cleaner at 16px favicon size
```

**Variant B — Full knot (more complex):**
```
- Six petals weave over/under each other
- Central hexagonal hole
- More detailed, better at 512px+
```

**Variant C — Adapted for your brand:**
Replace 6-fold with:
- 4-fold symmetry → clover/plus shape
- 8-fold → asterisk/burst
- 3-fold → triquetra/trinity knot

## Example Fill

```
[ICON_COLOR]        = #FFFFFF
[BACKGROUND_COLOR]  = #10A37F
```

**Expected:** Green squircle with white 6-petal knot flower, geometric precision, no gradients.

## ChatGPT-Specific Tips

1. **Don't mention "ChatGPT"** — describe the shape instead
2. **If output looks like a leaf:** add "geometric, mathematical, not organic"
3. **If overlapping is wrong:** add "petals weave over-under like Celtic knot"
4. **For readability at small sizes:** use Variant A (simple flower)
